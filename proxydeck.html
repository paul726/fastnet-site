FastNet

Use your iPhone's cellular connection on your Mac — over USB or Wi-Fi.

FastNet has two parts: a small app on your iPhone and the FastNet Connect app on your Mac. Together they route your Mac's traffic out through your iPhone's cellular data — so your Mac gets online through the phone, without burning the metered hotspot allowance.

Setup

  1. Install FastNet on your iPhone using the TestFlight invite that came with your purchase.
  2. Install FastNet Connect on your Mac and activate it with your license key.
  3. Connect the iPhone to the Mac with a USB cable, or put both on the same Wi-Fi.
  4. Open FastNet on the iPhone, then click Connect in the Mac app.

Privacy
