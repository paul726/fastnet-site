ProxyDeck

A private, on-device SOCKS5 proxy for iPhone — with a live connection inspector.

ProxyDeck runs a SOCKS5 proxy listener on your device and forwards traffic from any app you point at it — straight to the destination. Nothing is routed through anyone else's servers, and it collects no data.

How to use it

  1. Open ProxyDeck and tap Start. The proxy listens locally on 127.0.0.1:1082.
  2. Point any SOCKS5-aware app at 127.0.0.1, port 1082.
  3. Watch each connection appear in the live inspector — host, port, protocol, and status.
  4. Tap Stop to shut the proxy down.
