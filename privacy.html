Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 5, 2026

FastNet (iOS) and FastNet Connect (macOS) (“the Apps”) are developed by an independent developer. This Privacy Policy describes how your information is handled.

Data Collection

The Apps do not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal data. Specifically:

  • No analytics or tracking — no usage data is collected.
  • No accounts or registration — the Apps require no sign-in.
  • No network data logging — traffic passing through the proxy is relayed directly and is never stored, inspected, or recorded.
  • No third-party SDKs — the Apps contain no advertising, analytics, or crash-reporting frameworks.

How the Apps Work

The Apps function as a local proxy. Network traffic is forwarded in real time between the local device and the destination server. No data is cached, logged, or retained after transmission, and none of it passes through any server operated by us.

Data Storage

The Apps store only user-configurable settings (such as a port number) locally on the device. No data is transmitted to external servers.

Children’s Privacy

The Apps do not collect any personal information from anyone, including children under 13.

Changes

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. Changes will be posted to this page.
